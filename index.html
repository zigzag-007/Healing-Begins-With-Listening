About Us

At "Healing Begins with Listening", we believe in the transformative power of compassionate listening. Our mission is to provide a safe and supportive environment where individuals can share their stories and find healing.

  • Creating a community where your voice is heard, your feelings are validated, and your journey is shared through thoughtful words.
  • A safe space for teens to talk openly, find a listening ear, and receive heartfelt advice. Your journey is our story.
  • Offering a variety of services including individual counseling, group therapy, workshops, and online support groups.
